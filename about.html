My Story

Current

Currently I am working as a freelance mural artist. During quarantine I began designing and painting murals in bedrooms. I have since started painting locally in my hometown Letterkenny. My latest mural is the angel wings mural for Mc Ginley’s Bar Letterkenny.

Background

After graduating with a BA honours degree in Costume design, I began working as a freelance seamstress. My first career opportunity was as a seamstress assistant to Gabriella Slade for the ‘Spice World Tour’ in Dublin Ireland. While working here I learned valuable skills in gem stoning onto garments and shoe embellishing. I later worked as a touring seamstress and costume assistant in theatre on the Angela’s Ashes production in 2019. During my employment I made costumes for stage and altered existing costumes for cast. My role also included fittings, dressing, and maintenance.
