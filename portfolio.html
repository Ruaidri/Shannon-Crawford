01

  • Alice in Wonderland

    Costume Design

  • Date

    2018

CONCEPT

My graduation exhibition project was Alice in Wonderland, where I created Alice’s costume in the Victorian period with a stylised, contemporary twist, using modern fabrics. The image depicts Alice in a vulnerable world consumed by fear within her mind. The vision for this piece was adapted from the script Tim Burton’s Alice in Wonderland, set in Victorian London from 1855 to 1865.Young Alice grieves the loss of her father from an early age and carrys this grief through adolescence.Wonderland is Alice’s state of mind. A series of events that take place in her life in London cause her to spiral down the rabbit hole. It is in Wonderland that Alice learns to overcome the issues she faces in her real world through the strange characters she meets. This piece was art directed by Shannon Crawford. Production designer Aidian forde collaborated on the piece and was photographed by Peter Evers.

03

  • Jungle Bedroom

    Bedroom Mural

  • Date

    2020

CONCEPT

During quarantine my ten year old sister asked me to design her a jungle themed bedroom. While researching I stumbled upon the work of Henri Rousseau. I was totally inspired by his wonderful use of colour and how he portrayed the animals in the original painting. I am paying tribute to his painting 'Tropical Forest with Monkeys 1910. I wanted to design a bedroom suitable for a ten year old, with lots of colour, texture, plants and animals.
